SushiRoll: The Game

SushiRoll is an endless iOS 3D game avaiable on the App Store. With its intuitive mechanics and cartoonish graphics it is meant for everybody, from a casual gamer to the most competitive one. For this project I made all the 3d models and approached the world of project management by holding standup meetings at the beginning of the week and retrospective meetings at the end of the week, plus one short, daily meeting to keep the team in sync and on track. I used Miro and created a sprint backlog to organise time, divide and manage tasks.
